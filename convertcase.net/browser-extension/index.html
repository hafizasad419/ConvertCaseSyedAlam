Convert Case Browser Extensions

We have developed a browser extension so you can transform text between cases directly in your browser. Simply install it from your browser extension store, right click text and it'll be transformed directly in place. Optionally you can set it to auto copy the result to your clipboard for easy copy & pasting.

What is the Change Case Browser Extension?

The Convert Case Browser Extension is a tool we've developed to allow users to easily transform text between various cases directly in their browser. By simply right-clicking the text, users can convert it in place without needing to go to another tool or website.

How do I install the Convert Case Browser Extension?

You can install the Convert Case Browser Extension from your respective browser's extension store. Once installed, you can immediately start using it.

Which devices or browsers are compatible with this extension?

The extension is designed to be compatible with most major browsers (Chrome, Firefox and Microsoft Edge). Please check the listing in your browser's extension store to ensure compatibility with your specific device and browser version.

How does the extension work?

Once installed, highlight the text you wish to convert in your browser, right-click on it, and choose the desired case conversion option. The text will be transformed directly in place.

What types of text transformations does the extension support?

The extension supports a variety of case conversions, including UPPER CASE, lower case, Sentence case, Title Case, and also a Slugify option.

What is the 'Copy After Conversion' option in the context menu?

The 'Copy After Conversion' option allows users to automatically copy the converted text to their clipboard after the conversion. This is particularly useful for users who want to easily copy and paste the converted text elsewhere.

How do I set the extension to auto copy the result to my clipboard?

After right-clicking the selected text, choose the 'Copy After Conversion' option in the context menu. This will ensure the converted text is automatically copied to your clipboard.

Are there any costs associated with using the extension?

The Convert Case Browser Extension is free to use. We believe in enhancing user productivity without adding to their costs.

Can I provide feedback or report issues with the extension?

Yes, we value user feedback. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please reach out through the support link provided in the extension's store listing.

Does the extension store or transmit my data?

Our extension is designed with user privacy in mind. It does not store or transmit your data to any external servers. All conversions are done locally on your device.
